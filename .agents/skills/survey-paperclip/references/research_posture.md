# Research posture for this workflow

Use this workflow for rigorous academic research assistance, not for generic essay generation.

Operational principles:

1. Source fidelity is more important than fluency.
2. A clean outline does not compensate for thin evidence.
3. Every important citation must be read enough to explain its technical contribution.
4. Preprints and recent papers should be handled with status labels, not discarded.
5. Search should be iterative and adversarial: after drafting, actively look for missing concepts and papers.
6. Unsupported claims should be removed, weakened, or backed by additional evidence.
7. The final artifact should be auditable through search logs, evidence cards, claim audit, and validation report.
